Musician of The Day: Dave Alvin

Dave Alvin is a famous American singer who was born on November 11, 1955 in Downey, California. He can play the guitar and also writes songs. He was one of the first people to create a band called the Blasters, and he has also played in two other bands named X and the Knitters. When Dave was young, he and his older brother, Phil, loved listening to rock and country music. They started their own band in 1979 and Dave was the main songwriter and lead guitarist. He made the band very popular as they played many of his own songs. However, in 1986, Dave decided to leave the band because he want to sing his own songs. But he still joins the Blasters for special performances.
