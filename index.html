Musician of the Day: Patti Smith

Patti Smith is a lady who wears many hats! She was born in 1946 and grew up in New Jersey. Patti is a singer, songwriter, poet, painter, and also writes books. She is very popular because of her mix of rock music and poetry. She made a famous song called "Because the Night" with Bruce Springsteen that lots of people loved. She even got an award from France for her contributions to the arts. In 2007, she got added to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame which is a really big honor for musicians. A few years later, she won an award for a book she wrote, which was a promise she had made to her friend, Robert Mapplethorpe. People think she's a great artist too - she's on Rolling Stone magazine's list of the best artists ever!
