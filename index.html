Musician of the Day: Hayley Williams

Hayley Williams is a famous singer from America. She was born on December 27, 1988, and is really well known for being the lead singer in a rock band called Paramore. Hayley also plays the keyboards in the band. Throughout her career with Paramore, they have made six albums and she has been the only member to be in all of them. Hayley didn't just sing with Paramore though, she also did other music projects. One of them was a song called "Teenagers" for a movie. She also worked with other bands and singers, like B.o.B., helping them with their songs. One of the songs she helped with even reached No.2 on the U.S. music charts!
