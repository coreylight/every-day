Musician of the day: Babydaddy

The article is about a man named Scott Hoffman, but he is more commonly known as Babydaddy. He is a musician who plays many instruments and sings backup for a band named Scissor Sisters. He is really good at making music, which is why he won a special award called the Ivor Novello. Babydaddy was born in Texas but grew up in Kentucky. After high school, he went to Columbia University where he learned about writing and making dance music. Babydaddy knew a guy named Jake Shears when they were kids, and when Babydaddy moved to New York for college, they decided to start a band together! This was the start of Scissor Sisters in 2001, with Babydaddy making the music and Shears singing and performing. The band's logo was even designed by Babydaddy. Over the years, more people joined their band and they have gotten bigger and more popular.
