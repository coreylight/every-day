Artist of the day: Rickie Lee Jones

Rickie Lee Jones is a singer, musician, and songwriter who was born on November 8, 1954. She has been making music for more than 50 years in many styles like rock, R&B, pop, soul, and jazz. Rickie Lee has won two Grammy Awards and was named one of the 100 Greatest Women in Rock & Roll. Her song "Makin' Whoopee" with another singer, Dr. John, won a Grammy for Best Jazz Vocal Performance. She took a little break from making music but came back with more good songs. Rickie Lee has been nominated for seven Grammy Awards over her career.
