Animal of the Day: Nilgai Antelope

Habitat: Nilgai Antelope are found in India, Nepal, and Pakistan. They are primarily found in forests, grasslands, and shrublands.
Diet: They are herbivores and primarily feed on grass, leaves, fruits, and agricultural crops.
Interesting Fact: Nilgai Antelope are the largest Asian antelope species, known for their distinctive blue-gray color.
