Musician of the Day: Billy Rush

Billy Rush on Wikipedia

Billy Rush, also known as William Rush or Little Willie Rush, is an American musician, guitarist, composer, producer, arranger, and engineer. He has had a lengthy association with Southside Johnny and the Asbury Jukes from 1975 to 1985 and has also worked with Serge Gainsbourg as a guitarist and musical director from 1984 to 1991. In addition, Rush was a member of the hard rock band Liddle, Rush and Thrall in 2002. Throughout his career, Rush has worked as a composer, producer, and engineer for various artists, including Muddy Waters and John Hammond. He currently resides in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.
