Musician of the Day: Rita Reys

Rita Reys was a very famous jazz singer from the Netherlands. People called her "Europe's First Lady of Jazz". She was born in 1924 and she passed away in 2013. She made lots of great music in her life and people really loved to hear her singing. In the 1980s, she recorded some albums with the Lex Jasper orchestra. Albums are like books filled with songs. Some of her albums had really interesting names like "The Cool Voice of Rita Reys" and "Rita Sings Burt Bacharach". In 2009, she performed at a place called the Amsterdam Concertgebouw and everyone clapped so much they stood up. It was like receiving an amazing gold star at school for doing a great job!
