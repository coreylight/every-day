Animal of the Day: The Blue Tit

This bird loves mixed and deciduous woods - but can also be found in parks and gardens. The blue tit eats insects, caterpillars, seeds and nuts.

Musician of the Day: Lists of musicians by birthday

This is about a big list on the internet that tells you when different musicians have their birthdays. There are about 365 pages in this list. These pages are sorted by the date, like April 1 or August 15, so you can look up to see which musicians have the same birthday as you! The list helps people learn about music and musicians. It's split up by days of the year, so you can find out which musicians were born on the same day as you. Isn't that fun?
