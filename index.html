Musician of the day:

John Coltrane was a famous American jazz musician who played the saxophone. He was born in 1926 in North Carolina and died in 1967 in New York. He made a big impact on the world of jazz and lots of people still talk about his music today. John moved to Philadelphia after high school to study music. He was known for creating new ways to play jazz and he made many recordings. One of his most popular albums is called "A Love Supreme." Even after he died, people still remember his music and he has won many awards. The African Orthodox Church even made him a saint!
