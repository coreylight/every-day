Animal of the Day: Barn Owl

The Barn Owl is a nocturnal bird found almost everywhere in the world except for the polar and desert regions. They prefer to live in open farmland but can be found in a wide range of habitats. Their diet mainly consists of small mammals like mice and rats.

Musician of the Day: Frans Brüggen

Frans Brüggen was a man from the Netherlands who played and taught music. He was known for playing the flute and recorder, and was really good at it too! He even helped start a special group called Sour Cream where they played recorders in unique ways. Frans also helped create an orchestra, which is a big group of people playing music together. He was the main person leading this orchestra for a long time. Additionally, he worked with other orchestras and conducted, or led, them in playing music. He loved music and worked with it until he passed away in 2014.

Word of the Day: Elephant

An elephant is a large mammal that is known for its long trunk, tusks, and large ears.

Usage: The elephant is the largest animal that lives on land.
