Musician of the Day - Greyson Chance

Greyson Chance is an American singer-songwriter and musician. He gained national attention in 2010 when a video of him performing Lady Gaga's "Paparazzi" at a music festival went viral on YouTube. Chance has released several singles and albums, including his debut studio album "Hold On 'til the Night" in 2011 and his second full-length album "Portraits" in 2019. Born in 1997 in Wichita Falls, Texas, Chance grew up in Edmond, Oklahoma. He started playing the piano at a young age and had several years of lessons before his viral success. Chance's music covers genres such as pop, pop rock, electronic, and dance-pop. He has a YouTube channel with nearly 900,000 subscribers and over 200 million total views.
