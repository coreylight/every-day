Musician of the Day - Ann Reinking

Ann Reinking was a great dancer, actor, choreographer, and singer. She was born in Seattle and grew up in Bellevue. She won awards and was famous for her work in musical theater and movies. As a kid, Ann started ballet classes and even danced with a big English ballet company when she was only 12! She kept studying dance even while going to middle school and high school. After she finished high school, she continued her dance training with the Joffrey Ballet. She passed away in December 2020, but she will be remembered for her many talents.
