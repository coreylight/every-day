Musician of the Day: Jo-el Sonnier

Jo-El Sonnier is an American musician who plays the accordion and sings country and Cajun music. He was born on October 2, 1946, and started playing music when he was very young. He's made more than thirty albums and some of his songs were very popular. He is still making music today. Jo-El was born in a place called Rayne, Louisiana. He started playing his brother's accordion when he was only three years old and had started recording songs by the time he was eleven. He has played on many different music records and even got nominated for a big award called a Grammy. Even though he didn't always sell lots of albums, he never stopped making music.
