Musician of the Day: Brad Roberts

Brad Roberts is a famous singer and guitar player from Canada. He was born on January 10, 1964 and he is the lead singer of a band called Crash Test Dummies. The band is known for their song "Mmm Mmm Mmm Mmm". Brad is the only person who has been in the band the whole time it has existed. Brad started his music career after he graduated from the University of Winnipeg in 1986. He started playing music with his brother Dan in a band at a cafe in Winnipeg. Later, this band became the Crash Test Dummies. Brad also wrote his own songs while he was studying at university and working as a bartender.
