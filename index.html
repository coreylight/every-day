Animal of the Day: Jellyfish!

Habitat: Ocean

Diet: Plankton

Jellyfish are fascinating creatures that can be found in oceans around the world. They have a gelatinous body and their tentacles contain venomous cells that they use to capture prey. They are known for their pulsating movements and beautiful translucent appearance.
