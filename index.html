Kristin Hersh

Kristin Hersh is an American singer, guitarist, and songwriter known for her solo work and as a member of the bands Throwing Muses and 50FootWave. She has released eleven solo albums and her music ranges from alternative rock to folk and pop. Hersh's lyrics are known for their stream-of-consciousness style. Hersh was born in Atlanta, Georgia, and moved to Newport, Rhode Island, at a young age. She became interested in music early on and started playing guitar when she was nine. She formed the band Throwing Muses in high school, where she wrote and sang most of their songs. Hersh's musical career started early and has spanned several decades.
