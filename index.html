The Animal of the Day is the Lion. Lions live in grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands. They are carnivorous animals and feed on various animals like zebras, antelopes, and wildebeest. Lions are known as the "king of the jungle." They are social animals and live in groups called prides.

Musician of the Day: Christiane Legrand

Christiane Legrand was a French soprano and singer. She was born in Paris in 1930 and began studying music at a young age. Legrand became the lead singer in notable French jazz vocal groups in the 1960s and was the original lead soprano of The Swingle Singers. She also provided vocals for films and worked on various music projects. Legrand passed away in 2011 at the age of 81.
