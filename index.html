Fun Facts of the Day - July 26, 2023

Animal of the Day: Elephant

The elephant is the largest land animal on Earth. They can weigh up to 6 tonnes and grow as tall as 13 feet. Elephants have a long trunk that they use for breathing, smelling, drinking, and grabbing objects. They are herbivores and mainly eat leaves, fruits, and grass. Elephants are social creatures and live in groups called herds. They can be found in various habitats including forests, savannahs, and grasslands.

Music Artist: Harry Styles

Harry Styles is a popular music artist. He was born in 1994 in Redditch, Worcestershire, England. Styles gained fame as a member of the boy band One Direction. After the band's hiatus, he launched a successful solo career. His musical genres include pop, rock, and folk. Styles is known for his unique fashion sense and charismatic performances.
