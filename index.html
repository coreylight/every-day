Musician of the Day: Mike Pinera

Mike Pinera is a famous guitarist, singer, and songwriter. He was born on September 29, 1948, in Tampa, Florida. He started playing music when he was very young and has been a part of many music bands. Some of the bands he was part of include Blues Image, Ramatam, New Cactus, and even played guitar for Alice Cooper. Mike is actively playing music and performs with his own band and a group called The Classic Rock All-Stars. Mike Pinera and his band, the Blues Image, were very popular. They even had a big song called "Ride Captain Ride". Mike was also the first to use something called a "Guitar Talk Box" in a song called "Butterfly Bleu". He formed many bands with other musicians and continued making music throughout his life. He still makes music today and works with other musicians to put on concerts.
