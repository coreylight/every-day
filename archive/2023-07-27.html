Animal of the Day: Dolphin

Dolphins are marine mammals known for their intelligence and playful behavior. They are found in oceans all around the world. Dolphins are highly social animals and live in groups called pods. They communicate using a series of clicks, whistles, and body movements.

Habitat: Dolphins can be found in both warm and cold waters. They usually prefer coastal areas but are also present in deeper offshore waters.
