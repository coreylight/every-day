Word of the Day: Caterpillar

Caterpillar is the name for the larval stage of a butterfly or moth's life cycle. Most caterpillars are herbivores, but a few are carnivorous and even cannibalistic.

Example: The caterpillar eats the leaf to get energy to turn into a butterfly.
