Musician of the Day: Martin Jackson

Martin Jackson is a famous drummer from England. He has played music with many bands, but people know him best for his work with the bands Magazine and Swing Out Sister. He played drums on Magazine's "Real Life" album in 1978 and hit song "Breakout" with Swing Out Sister in 1986. Martin started his music career in the 1970s with bands like The Bees Knees and The Freshies. He joined the band Magazine and they became very popular. However, he left the band in 1978. In the 1980s, he played with other bands like The Chameleons and Design 9. Then, he joined Swing Out Sister and played on their first album. But he left the band while they were making their second album.
