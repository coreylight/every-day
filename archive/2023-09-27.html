Musician of the Day: Carlene Carter

Carlene Carter is a well-known American country music singer and songwriter. She was born in Tennessee and started making music in 1978. She's the daughter of June Carter Cash and Carl Smith, who are also famous singers. Carlene has made twelve albums with many popular songs in her career. One of her first recordings was on an album by her stepdad, Johnny Cash. She began her own music career in the late 1970s. She's performed at big concerts, and launched lots of successful songs, including one about mate-swapping called "Swap-Meat Rag." Her music career is still ongoing!
