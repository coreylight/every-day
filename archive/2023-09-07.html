Musician of the Day: Roger Waters

Roger Waters is a famous musician from England. He helped start a very popular rock band called Pink Floyd in 1965. Roger played the bass guitar and wrote a lot of the words to their songs. Some of their best known albums are The Dark Side of the Moon and The Wall. In 1985, Roger stopped being a part of Pink Floyd and made music on his own. He had some arguments about who got to use the name Pink Floyd, but they figured it out without going to court. Roger has also made his own albums like Radio K.A.O.S and Is This the Life We Really Want?. He even made an opera about the French Revolution!
