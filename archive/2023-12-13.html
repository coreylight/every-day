Musician of the Day: Eric Schenkman

The Spin Doctors is a rock band from New York City. They are famous for their songs "Two Princes" and "Little Miss Can't Be Wrong" from the early 1990s. The current members of the band are Chris Barron, Eric Schenkman, and Aaron Comess. The Spin Doctors started in the late 1980s and were first called the Trucking Company. The group included Eric Schenkman, John Popper, and later Chris Barron. John Popper left to focus on his band Blues Traveler. Then the band changed their name to Spin Doctors, added Aaron Comess and Mark White, and started making music together.
