Musician of the Day: Dick Smothers

Dick Smothers is an actor, comedian, musician, and composer. He was born in New York City in 1938. His mom was a homemaker and his dad was an army officer who, sadly, passed away as a prisoner of war. Dick and his older brother Tom used to perform together as the Smothers Brothers. They were really funny and played music together, too! Dick and his brother Tom had their own television shows called The Smothers Brothers Show, and The Smothers Brothers Comedy Hour. They were on TV a lot and people thought they were hilarious. Dick even did some acting by himself. In 1993, he played a character in a Christmas cartoon movie. Then in 1995, he acted in a serious movie called Casino. Dick has had an exciting career in entertainment!
