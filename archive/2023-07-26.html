Fun Facts of the Day - July 26, 2023

Animal of the Day: Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth. They have long necks that can reach up to 6 feet in length. They live in grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands in Africa.

Color of the Day: Sunny Yellow (#FFD700)

Sunny Yellow (#FFD700)

Music Artist of the Day: Christopher Cross

Christopher Cross is an American singer-songwriter born in 1951. He is known for his soft rock and pop music. Cross was born in San Antonio, Texas, and gained popularity in the early 1980s. His most famous song is "Sailing."

Word of the Day: Adventure

Adventure means an exciting or daring experience. "I went on an adventure and discovered a hidden treasure in the forest!" said Tim.
