Musician of the Day: Jimmy Butts

Jimmy Butts was a famous jazz player who played a big instrument called a double-bass. He played with a lot of different bands and even played music for soldiers during a war. He later moved to Canada and played music there, but then he moved back to New York City and started a small band of his own. Even after Jimmy passed away, his band kept playing music and named themselves "Friends of Jimmy Butts" to remember him. Jimmy's music was so special, there is a special collection of it at a university library.
