Musician of the Day: Ric Lee

Ric Lee is a famous English drummer who was born on October 20, 1945. He is known for playing in a band called Ten Years After. Ric started his music career in a band called The Falcons. He later joined other bands including The Jaybirds and The Mansfields. In 1966, he moved to London and joined a band that later became known as Ten Years After. Ten Years After became very successful and played at big music festivals like Woodstock in 1969 and the Isle of Wight Festival in 1970. When they stopped playing in 1976, Ric Lee started his own music business. He helped other musicians to publish their songs and manage their careers.
