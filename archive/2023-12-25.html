Musician of the Day: Woody Shaw

Woody Herman Shaw Jr. was a famous trumpet player who made lots of changes in jazz music. He was born on December 24th, 1944 in North Carolina and died on May 10th, 1989 in New York City. Besides being a great musician, Woody Shaw also taught music and led a music band. When Woody was only a year old, his family moved to Newark, New Jersey. His dad was in a singing group and because of that, Woody was introduced to music at a very young age. He grew up to be one of the most important jazz musicians of his time, working with other famous musicians and teaching others how to play music.
