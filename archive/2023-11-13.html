Booker T. Jones is a musician from Memphis, Tennessee. He's been making music since 1962 and is most famous for being the leader of the band Booker T. & the M.G.'s. He has also made music with lots of other famous musicians and for his excellent work, he was given a special award called the Grammy Award for lifetime achievement. Booker T. Jones was born on November 12, 1944, and he was named after his dad who was a science teacher. As a kid, he was very good at playing lots of different instruments like the oboe, saxophone, trombone, double bass, and piano. He even played the organ at church! He went to the same high school as other famous musicians like Rufus Thomas and Isaac Hayes's writing partner David Porter.
