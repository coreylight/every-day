Musician of the Day: Chauncey Black

Blackstreet is a music group from New York City. They sing R&B music and were created in 1991 by producers named Chauncey "Black" Hannibal and Teddy "Street" Riley. They have made four albums and their most popular song is called "No Diggity." The group was first made up of Hannibal, Riley, and two other guys named Joseph Stonestreet and Levi Little. They were going to call the group Stonestreet, but then changed it to Blackstreet. After their first song "Baby Be Mine" was released, Stonestreet left and was replaced by a man named Dave Hollister. Their first album had three popular songs called "Booti Call", "Before I Let You Go" and "Joy". "Before I Let You Go" was a big hit and made it to the Top 10 songs. Their second album was released in 1996.
