Musician of the Day: Hikaru Utada

Hikaru Utada is a singer, songwriter, and music producer from Japan and America. She was born on January 19, 1983, in New York City to Japanese parents. She started writing songs at a young age and began her singing career in Japan during the late 1990s. Even though her first album in English didn't do very well, her first album in Japanese, called "First Love," was very successful and is now the best-selling album in Japan. Utada's second album, "Distance," was released in 2001 and had some of her biggest songs. Even though she took a break from singing for a few years, she started again in 2016. Many people in Japan really love her music.
