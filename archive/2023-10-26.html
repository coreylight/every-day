Musician of the Day: Matthias Jabs

Matthias Jabs is a very talented guitar player from Germany who is famous for playing in a rock band called Scorpions. He started playing with them in 1979 and has played on almost all of their albums. He also owns his own music store in Munich, Germany, called MJ Guitars. Before he joined the Scorpions, Matthias played in other bands like Lady, Fargo, and Deadlock. He got the chance to join the Scorpions when their old guitar player, Uli Roth, quit the band. Uli wasn't happy with the direction the band was going, so he left to start his own band. Then, the bass player of the Scorpions, who had been Matthias' math tutor, recommended him for the job.
