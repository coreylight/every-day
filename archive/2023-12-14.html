Musician of the Day: Carlos Montoya

Carlos Garcia Montoya was a famous musician from Madrid, Spain, who played flamenco music on the guitar. He was born in 1903 and passed away in 1993. As a young boy, Carlos learned how to play guitar from his mom and a local barber. He got really good and by the time he was 14, he was performing in music cafes. In the 1920s and 1930s, Carlos traveled all over the world and played music with other musicians. When a big war started in 1939, Carlos was visiting the United States and he decided to stay and live in New York City. He became a U.S. citizen and during this time, he started to play styles of music like blues, jazz, and folk in addition to flamenco.
